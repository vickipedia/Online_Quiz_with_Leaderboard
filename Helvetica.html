Answers

Question 1: Ladakh
The Indian Astronomical Observatory (IAO), located near Leh in Ladakh, India, has one of the world's highest sites for optical, infrared and gamma-ray telescopes. It is operated by the Indian Institute of Astrophysics, Bangalore. It is currently the second highest optical telescope in the world, situated at an elevation of 4,500 meters (14,764 ft).

Question 2: Ben Kingsley
Ben Kingsley is an Indian-born, Hollywood actor from United Kingdom. He is known for his starring role as Mohandas Gandhi in the 1982 film Gandhi, for which he won the Academy Award for Best Actor.

Question 3: Enchanting Tamil Nadu
"Enchanting Tamil Nadu" is the official slogan of Tamil Nadu Tourism Development Corporation. The slogan was officially adopted in 2003.
